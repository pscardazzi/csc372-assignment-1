Overview

Located in the northeast coast of Brazil, in the state of SÃ£o Paulo, Ubatuba holds a special place in my heart. I used to go here with my parents and family fairly often when I was younger, and I have a lot of fond memories of the place. If you're looking for some beachside fun, then this is a good place to visit!

Must-See Locales

  1. Felix Beach: There are many beaches in Ubatuba, but this one takes the cake. A big beach with rowdy seas, though it comes with a nice, mellow river as a relaxing sidepiece!
  2. Selinha Island: (Probably) the smallest island in the world! Take a boat ride, or go snorkel diving there is you're experienced!
  3. Ubatuba Aquarium: A 29-year old aquarium with over 400 animals and 110 species!
  4. TAMAR Project: See the fruits of labor of an organization seeking to protect sea turtles!
  5. Rei do Peixe: Enjoy Brazil's take on seafood in this great restaurant!

Travel Tips

  • Bring lots of sunscreen!
  • Check the weather before going for your vacation!
  • Set aside a reasonable budget for your ventures!
  • Being in a good headspace is very important!
Ubatuba is a place that I hold near and dear to my heart. I have so many good memories of this place, and I think you can make some good memories here too!
